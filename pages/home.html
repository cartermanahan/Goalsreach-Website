FAQ

What is Goalsreach?

Goalsreach is a gamified accountability app built around goals, habits, Accountability Points, and rewards you define for yourself.

How do rewards work?

You decide the AP price of every reward, then spend your earned AP whenever you want to redeem it. The system only works if you stay honest with yourself.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Monthly comes with a 3-day free trial and yearly comes with a 7-day free trial.

Why does it work better than a normal tracker?

Because it gives progress visible weight. Goals earn AP, habits can earn AP, and rewards cost AP. It stops being a passive tracker and becomes a loop you can actually feel.
